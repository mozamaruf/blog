MAPS ANALYSIS & DESIGN

PITTSBURGH

ABOUT THE PROJECT

The project looks into the correlation of obesity with active lifestyle and unhealthy eating in Pittsburgh area. Geographic Area: Pittsburgh, Allegheny County, Pennsylvania, USA
Specific purpose & objective:
1. Which neighborhoods in Pittsburgh are better to live in for healthy lifestyle?
2. How fast food impacts obesity and lifestyle of the people in different areas (neighbourhoods) of Pittsburgh.
3. Linking recreational facilities/gyms to unhealthy lifestyle.
4. Finally, correlation between the ratio of fast food and recreational facilities/gym with unhealthy lifestyle

Tools used for the project includes: ArcMap GIS, Arc Catalog
